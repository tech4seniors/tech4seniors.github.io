About Us


What we do

We partner with Toronto organizations involved in senior care to find and implement innovative technological solutions to important problems. We know there are a lot of problems in this industry for many different stakeholders, and we want to help relieve some of these issues.

What makes us different

We are a group of technologists and engineers dedicated to improving senior care. With our knowledge of software and current technologies, we devise practical solutions that can be implemented immediately.

Technologies

Which

Generally we integrate free or low-cost technologies to create new solutions. We also implement artificial intelligence technologies to deal with certain issues.

Concerns

There are a lot of restrictions for technologies around healthcare, safety and privacy being the most important. We work within the bounds of these restrictions to deliver solutions so that no one gets hurt.
